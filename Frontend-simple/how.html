How FoodShare works

FoodShare connects PGs / donors who have surplus cooked food with NGOs that can quickly collect and distribute it to people in need. Here is the full journey:

Step 1 · PG / Donor

Sign up & login

PG owners or students create a donor account and log in to the system. NGOs create a receiver account to claim food.

Step 2 · Upload

Post surplus food

PG posts: food title, description, quantity, location and food type (Veg / Non-Veg / Mixed). The post becomes visible to all NGOs.

Step 3 · Discover

NGOs browse available food

NGOs log in and open the "Available Food" page. They can see all current posts along with quantity and pickup location.

Step 4 · Claim

NGO claims a post

When an NGO clicks "Claim", the system marks that food as claimed, and the donor can prepare the food for pickup.

Step 5 · Pickup

Pickup & distribution

The NGO reaches the PG at the specified pickup location and time, collects the food, and distributes it to needy people.

Impact

Saving food · Fighting hunger

This simple workflow reduces food wastage in PGs and hostels, while supporting local communities and aligning with social responsibility goals.
